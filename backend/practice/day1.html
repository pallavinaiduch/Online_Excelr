Introduction to HTML Headings

There are six types of headings in HTML, each with a different level of importance and size. The types of headings are:

  • h1: Main title
  • h2: Subtitle
  • h3: Sub-subtitle
  • h4: Sub-sub-subtitle
  • h5: Sub-sub-sub-subtitle
  • h6: Sub-sub-sub-sub-subtitle
